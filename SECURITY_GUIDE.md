# 🔒 Security Guide: Making Discipline App Tamper-Resistant

## ⚠️ **Current Limitations**

**Safari Content Blocker alone is NOT foolproof.** Here's what users can currently do to bypass:

### ❌ **Easy Bypasses:**
- **Delete the app** → Removes extension
- **Disable extension** in Settings > Safari > Content Blockers
- **Use Chrome/Firefox** → Only Safari is blocked
- **Use VPN** → Routes traffic around blocking
- **Factory reset device** → Removes everything

---

## 🛡️ **Enhanced Security Setup**

To make blocking **much harder to bypass**, follow these steps:

### **Step 1: Enable Screen Time Restrictions**

This is the **most important** security step:

1. **Settings** → **Screen Time** → **Turn On Screen Time**
2. **Set a Screen Time passcode** (DIFFERENT from device passcode!)
3. **Don't tell anyone the passcode** (write it down somewhere safe)

### **Step 2: Prevent App Deletion**

1. **Settings** → **Screen Time** → **Content & Privacy Restrictions**
2. **iTunes & App Store Purchases** → **Deleting Apps** → **Don't Allow**
3. Now the Discipline app **cannot be deleted**! ✅

### **Step 3: Prevent Settings Changes**

1. **Settings** → **Screen Time** → **Content & Privacy Restrictions**
2. **Allowed Changes** → **Safari** → **Don't Allow**
3. Now Safari settings **cannot be changed** (extension can't be disabled)! ✅

### **Step 4: Block Other Browsers (Optional)**

1. **Settings** → **Screen Time** → **App Limits**
2. **Add Limit** → **Other** → Select Chrome, Firefox, etc.
3. **Set limit to 1 minute** → **Block at End of Limit** ✅

### **Step 5: Prevent Screen Time Changes**

1. **Settings** → **Screen Time** → **Content & Privacy Restrictions**
2. **Allowed Changes** → **Screen Time** → **Don't Allow**
3. Now Screen Time settings **cannot be changed**! ✅

---

## 🔐 **Maximum Security Configuration**

After following all steps above:

### ✅ **What's Now Protected:**
- ✅ **App cannot be deleted** (Screen Time restriction)
- ✅ **Extension cannot be disabled** (Safari settings locked)
- ✅ **Other browsers blocked** (App Limits)
- ✅ **Screen Time cannot be changed** (Settings locked)
- ✅ **Timer cannot be stopped** (No cancel button in app)
- ✅ **Automatic expiration** (Timer-based)

### ❌ **Remaining Bypasses:**
- ❌ **Factory reset** (nuclear option, loses all data)
- ❌ **VPN usage** (advanced users)
- ❌ **Screen Time passcode guessing** (if too simple)
- ❌ **Using another device** (phone, computer, etc.)

## 🚨 **Emergency Recovery**

### **If You Need to Disable Before Timer Expires:**

#### **Option 1: Screen Time Passcode**
- Enter your Screen Time passcode
- Disable restrictions temporarily
- Delete app or disable extension

#### **Option 2: Factory Reset (Nuclear)**
- **Settings** → **General** → **Transfer or Reset iPhone** → **Erase All Content and Settings**
- ⚠️ **WARNING**: This deletes EVERYTHING on your device!

#### **Option 3: Wait for Timer**
- The safest option is to wait for the timer to expire naturally
- Use the time for self-reflection and building discipline

## 💡 **Pro Tips for Maximum Effectiveness**

### **Passcode Security:**
- Use a **complex Screen Time passcode** (not 1234 or your birthday)
- **Don't use your device passcode** (too easy to remember)
- **Write it down** and store it somewhere inconvenient to access
- Consider giving it to a **trusted friend** for accountability

### **Psychological Barriers:**
- The **friction** of multiple steps prevents impulsive bypassing
- **Screen Time restrictions** require deliberate action to bypass
- **Complex passcode** adds thinking time before bypassing

### **Additional Accountability:**
- Tell a **trusted friend** about your commitment
- Use **app usage tracking** to monitor your progress
- Set **rewards** for successfully completing blocking periods
